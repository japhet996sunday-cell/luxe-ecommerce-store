Our story

Started with one coat that wouldn't wear out.

LUXE began in 2019 with a single wool overcoat, made because nothing on the market held up past a season. That coat is still worn today. Every piece since has been held to the same standard: built to be worn hundreds of times, not photographed once.

We work with a small number of mills and tanneries in Italy, Portugal, and Turkey — no seasonal drops, no seventy-item collections. Fewer pieces, made properly.

Mission

Clothes should outlast the trend that sold them to you.

We measure success in how long a piece stays in rotation, not how fast it sells out. That means fewer releases, higher material cost, and a size run that fits real bodies rather than a sample rack.

What we hold to

Brand values

verified

Material honesty

Every fabric and leather is named and sourced on the product page — no vague "premium blend" labeling.

handyman

Built to repair

Pieces are constructed so a tailor can mend them, not just replace them. We publish care and repair guides for everything we sell.

balance

Fewer, better

We release in small batches instead of chasing seasonal cycles. When something sells out, it's gone until the next production run.

Versioning

Future releases will follow semantic versioning:

- Major version for significant changes or breaking updates.
- Minor version for new features and improvements.
- Patch version for bug fixes and small enhancements.

Examples:

- 1.0.1 – Bug fixes.
- 1.1.0 – New features.
- 2.0.0 – Major redesign or breaking changes.
